About Us

Leading the Way in International Education

Our Mission

21Knowledge is a company created in 2016. Its skilled founder has worked for more than two decades in the field of education and has been involved in the Erasmus+ universe, namely as coordinator of an Erasmus+ KA1 project awarded in 2016 in the category of Inspirational Project. With the motto "Learning for the 21st Century", this is a company of the present, without the handicaps of the past, where we provide tools to prepare European teachers for a future that happens today. 21Knowledge's trainers are enthusiastic educational agents, namely teachers with a broad experience and actively involved in 21st Century Skills. 21Knowledge's mission is to work towards innovation having in mind a school of success.
